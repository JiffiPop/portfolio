Starry Station

Starry Wi-Fi Station

S00111

Background

While on the job hunt, I stumbled across Starry Internet which is an internet provider.  I was thrilled to see that there was at least some competition against Charter Spectrum in certain areas.  To express my interest in working for the company, I decided to do my best to recreate their device called the Starry Wi-Fi Station.  There was a very limited amount of information available on the dimensions of the product, but I did my best in a short time frame.  For this project, I used SolidWorks to design the replica and used Cinema 4D to render the design.
